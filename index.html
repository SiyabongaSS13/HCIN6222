About Us

We aimed at facilitating connections among university/college students across different faculties and campuses. Recognizing the importance of student networking beyond disciplinary boundaries, our team embarked on a journey to create a platform that fosters collaboration, communication, and community among students. Through collective brainstorming sessions, iterative design processes, and shared feedback, we have crafted a website tailored to meet the diverse needs and preferences of our fellow students.
